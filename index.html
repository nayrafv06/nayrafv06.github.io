About Me

Hi! My name is Nayra Flores Valdivia I was born and raised in Mexico City for ten years, I immigrated to the U.S. at the age of ten and I have been living in Chula Vista California for seven years now.

I am driven to study engineering and focus on problem solving for space related projects and research such as faster spacecrafts and travel times. I hope that my future contributions to the space community can help solve problems for future generations of astronauts, researchers, and engineers.

Through my high school experience I also became aware of the disadvantages my community faces in regards to higher education, to help I became a part of different clubs and programs to help bridge educational gaps. If you would like to read more about my community involvement and educational endeavors, refer to the summer programs and clubs pages on this website. You can also find my resume, gmail, linkedin, and github on the contact button at the top right portion of the website.

P.S. I also have two cats and a dog, they are really energetic and I like to call them my babies.
